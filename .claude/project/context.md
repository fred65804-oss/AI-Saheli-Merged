# Project Context — AI Saheli for MoWCD

## Ministry Overview
**Ministry of Women & Child Development (MoWCD)**, Government of India, oversees welfare of ~500 million women and children across India through flagship national schemes.

## The Vision
An **Agentic AI Ecosystem** where citizens interact through familiar channels (WhatsApp, mobile app, web) in their own language to access government services. Multiple domain-specific AI agents activate based on citizen context to provide personalized, grounded guidance.

## Why This Matters
- Millions of women in rural India cannot navigate complex government scheme eligibility
- Field workers (AWWs, ANMs) are overburdened with manual data entry and reporting
- Rights and entitlements are poorly communicated at the last mile
- Grievances take months to resolve without intelligent triage

## Target Beneficiaries
| Segment | Who | Key Need |
|---|---|---|
| Women Citizens | Rural & urban women | Scheme eligibility, legal rights, safety helplines |
| Mothers & Pregnant Women | Antenatal / postnatal | Nutrition, vaccination, antenatal care |
| Children & Parents | 0–18 years | Growth milestones, protection, adoption |
| Anganwadi Workers (AWW) | ~1.4M field workers | Data entry, report generation, inspection support |
| Field Officers | CDPOs, supervisors | Monitoring, compliance, escalation |
| Policy Makers | Ministry officials | Real-time analytics, scheme performance |

## AI Service Channels (Proposed)
1. **WhatsApp Assistant** — highest reach for rural citizens, voice notes supported
2. **Mobile App** — branded "AI Saheli" app
3. **Web Portal** — desktop/kiosk access
4. **IVR / Voice Assistant** — for feature-phone users
5. **Call Center AI (181 / Helpline)** — AI-assisted triage
6. **Anganwadi App** — field worker specific
7. **Kiosk / CSC** — Common Service Centre deployment

## Stakeholders
- **Citizens / Beneficiaries** — primary users of the AI assistant
- **Ministry (MoWCD)** — client, ultimate decision-maker, analytics consumer
- **State WCD Departments** — implementation partners
- **Anganwadi Workers** — field workforce using Copilot
- **ANMs (Auxiliary Nurse Midwives)** — receive risk alerts from Maa Mitra
- **DCPU / CWC** — receive child protection case referrals

## Presentation Context
This demo was preceded by a concept presentation to MoWCD in June 2026. The working demo is needed to **visualize the concept more effectively** before presentation to senior Ministry leadership. The demo must cover Poshan, Vatsalya, and Mission Shakti specifically.

## What "Success" Looks Like for the Demo
- Senior leadership can *see* a citizen querying the WhatsApp bot in Hindi by voice
- The system correctly identifies eligibility, finds the nearest Anganwadi, and gives cited guidance
- Distress/safety queries visibly route to helplines without the AI counselling
- The Ministry analytics dashboard shows real-time citizen interaction insights
- The AI clearly states it is grounded in official government documents
